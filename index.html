Common Questions

How does it actually work?

We connect an AI assistant to your WhatsApp Business account. We train it on your business — what you sell, your hours, your pricing, common questions. When a customer messages, the AI responds automatically with accurate, helpful answers. It's like having a staff member who never sleeps.

What if it gives wrong information?

We monitor every conversation in real-time. If something goes wrong, we catch it and fix it — usually within minutes. You also get a weekly report with everything that happened, and the AI only answers questions we've trained it on. If something is out of scope, it hands off to you.

Will my customers know it's AI?

Some might guess, but most won't mind because they get an instant answer. If a customer asks, the AI can honestly respond that it's an assistant and offer to connect them to you. What matters is they get help immediately instead of waiting.

Can I stop anytime?

Yes. Month-to-month. No lock-in contracts. Just give us notice and we'll transition everything back to manual operation.

How long does setup take?

About 7 days. The WhatsApp approval takes a couple of days, then training and testing takes a few more. You're involved for a 30-minute discovery call and a 30-minute test session — then you're live.

I don't understand AI. Can I still use this?

You don't need to understand how it works — that's our job. You just need to know it works. Your customers get faster replies, you get more leads, and we handle everything technical.
